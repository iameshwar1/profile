Eshwara Prasath Ramesh

Welcome!

I work as a V2X Engineer at Eaton doing some cool stuff with EV Charging, grid infrastructure, embedded systems and application development. I am broadly interested in the area of System design, Embedded systems and understanding System Architecture. Prior to this, I worked as as Systems Architect at Atom Power Inc. At Atom Power, I helped design and develop the world's first solid state circuit breaker. I have also architected and worked on several projects such as automatic transfer switching, mono pole residential breakers, demand management and EV charging.

I am a hands-on leader with over nine years of experience in the design, implementation, and management of connected systems. Practical experience with software development life cycle, design, cloud solutions and distributed systems. Architected and Implemented several large commercial and industrial IoT and software systems. Built and managed high-performance cross-functional teams. Passionate about technology and the continuous education required to master new architectures, frameworks, and patterns.
